I am a first-year PhD student supervised by Paul Hilaire (Quriosity) and Michael Vasmer (COSMIQ team at Inria). My research interests are quantum error correction, quantum error mitigation and early fault-tolerant quantum computing. I graduated from Sorbonne Université in 2023 with a dual Bachelor degree in Physics and Computer Science, and in 2025 with a Master’s degree in Quantum Information (Physics Track).
